05

Voice & tone

Sound like

Pragmatic, direct, confident. Short sentences. Concrete numbers. Talk about the customer's outcome, not our features. A peer who respects your time — not a vendor.

Never sound like

Hype, jargon, or hedging. No "revolutionary," no "synergy," no walls of qualifiers. If a sentence could be on any competitor's site, rewrite it.

06

Narrative / POV

The 120-word
story spine

Revenue tools got powerful and got slow. The category leaders bolted on every feature, then priced and provisioned them for the Fortune 500 — six-week rollouts, implementation fees, modules you discover at renewal. Mid-market teams got stuck choosing between toys that don't go deep and enterprise suites they can't afford the time to turn on.

We think that trade-off is dead. The work — knowing which deals are real, where pipeline leaks, who needs coaching — is the same whether you're 30 reps or 3,000. Northpeak gives mid-market teams that depth on day one, self-serve, at a price that makes sense. Connect your CRM this morning; decide from real numbers this afternoon. No rollout. No sales call. Just the truth about your pipeline, today.

Use it for the homepage scroll, the founder/sales narrative, the "why we exist" section, and the opening of investor or partner decks.
