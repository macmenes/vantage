The gap we found

The teardown showed Cobalt and Orrery dominate broad terms ("revenue intelligence," "sales analytics") with 200+ articles each. Nobody owns the fast-setup, mid-market long tail — the queries with real buying intent.

Our wedge

Every piece proves the same-day claim with a concrete how, a teardown, or a real timeline. We can publish things the enterprise vendors structurally can't — because their setup genuinely takes six weeks.

Primary KPI

Pipeline-influenced content: organic sessions → trial starts. Secondary: rankings for the 18 backlog terms; assisted conversions from middle-funnel comparison pages.

The rule every piece follows

If a competitor's content team could publish it word-for-word, we don't write it. Each piece earns its place with a number, a teardown, a template, or a timeline only Northpeak can credibly claim.

Distribution & measurement

Every piece ships with
  • A LinkedIn post from the founder (the angle, not the link)
  • One slide added to the sales deck if it's a comparison
  • A snippet in the nurture email of the month (→ Lifecycle pack)
  • Internal links from at least two existing pages
Review cadence
  • Day 30: indexing + first rankings for the 9 target queries
  • Day 60: organic sessions → trial starts by piece
  • Day 90: re-score the backlog, promote the next 9, prune anything flat
Hand-offs

Comparison pages → Vantage Battlecard (keep claims in sync with sales). Each pillar's promise → Positioning Studio messaging house (same words everywhere). Nurture snippets → Lifecycle & Email.
