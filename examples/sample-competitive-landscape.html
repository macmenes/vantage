Executive Summary

5 rivals deep-dived
+ long tail
Biggest Threat

Cobalt — the category leader. Broadest platform, strongest brand, and the only rival running a serious paid-media engine across every channel. It sets the agenda the rest of the set reacts to.

Biggest Opening — White Space

Self-serve mid-market, onboarding-led. Cobalt and Orrery skew enterprise and heavy; Cinder is cheap but thin. Nobody owns "powerful revenue intelligence you can stand up in a day, without the enterprise tax." That is Northpeak's defensible lane.

  1. Own the time-to-value story. Make "live in a day" the hero message across the site, ads, and sales — it's the one axis where Northpeak beats every rival and the buyer feels it immediately.
  2. Close the review-proof gap. Northpeak has happy customers but a near-empty G2/Capterra presence — a direct conversion and battlecard liability. Run a structured review drive.
  3. Pick one content lane and win it. Don't out-volume Beacon — out-depth them on a single buyer-critical topic cluster tied to onboarding/activation.

vs. Cobalt
Sample · fictional

Quick take: The category leader — broadest platform, biggest brand. We meet them in every mid-market evaluation that "went up-market to look at the leader." We win on speed-to-value, price-to-value, and self-serve fit; they win on breadth and brand safety.

Their pitch (steelman)"The complete, enterprise-grade revenue platform — every integration, analyst-recognized, trusted by the biggest teams. One vendor for everything."

Why we win

  • Live in a day, not a quarter — self-serve onboarding vs a multi-week implementation. buyers feel time-to-value immediately
  • Price-to-value for mid-market — you pay for depth you'll use, not enterprise modules you won't.
  • No sales-gate to evaluate — start free, prove it yourself.

Where they win — & our response

  • Breadth & ecosystem. Response: most mid-market teams use a fraction of it — we cover what you actually need, faster.
  • Brand safety ("nobody got fired…"). Response: de-risk with a free self-serve trial — proof beats reputation.

"Ask them what real onboarding looks like — timeline, services fees, and who does the work."
"Ask which modules are included at your tier vs add-ons at renewal."
